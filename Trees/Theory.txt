Hierarchial Structure
At most 2 childs 
leaf nodes = no childs
link b/w nodes is edge 



height = longest path from a node to leaf 

level = how far a node is from leaf

subtree = any node together with all its decendents

Types:
 1. Full Binary Tree - Either 0 or 2 
 2. Complete - Left to right complete, may or maynot be filled the last level
 3. Perfect - All internal nodes must have 2 childs. and all leaves are at same level
 4. Balanced - the left and right heights are roughly balanced, height difference at every node is 1 
 5. Skewed - Left, Right, every node has only 1 child
 6. AVL Tree - Self balancing, height difference at every node is 1 

Preorder -> root, left right 
inorder -> left root right, always sorted order in bst
postorder -> left right root


Implement postorder from inorder + preorder
pre order tells us who is root
suppose pre order is - 1 2 4 5 3 6
suppose inorder is - 4 2 5 1 3 6

STEP 1 : First element in preorder = 1, so root = 1
STEP 2 : Find 1 in inorder - 4 2 5 | 1 | 3 6

Everything left of 1 is left subtree

Everything right of 1 is right subtree

LEFT SUBTREE

Inorder: 4 2 5

Next preorder root: 2

Find 2:
4 | 2 | 5

Left: 4
Right: 5

RIGHT SUBTREE

Inorder: 3 6
Preorder root: 3
Find 3: 3 | 6
Right child: 6



What is Diameter?
Diameter = Longest path between any two nodes.
